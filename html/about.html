About Syscon

SYSCON was established in year 2005, we have expertise in diverse fields of Information technology. Our strength is in our constantly growing team of project managers, engineers and technical experts who are distinguished by their strong problem-solving and cross-industry technical capabilities; combined with their hands-on experience thereby ensuring that our clients receive the most effective and professional service.
